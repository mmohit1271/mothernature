Greed – The New Religion

Dear Homo Sapiens,

Congratulations. You've built temples of concrete, pipelines of profit, and forests of currency. The rivers? Drained. The mountains? Mined. The forests? Flattened. But hey — you’ve got skyscrapers, right? After all, nothing says “progress” like choking your own air and bottling your own water. 🏦🌫️

I watched you trade thousand-year-old trees for luxury resorts. You poisoned lakes for mining gold you'll bury in a locker. You even put a price tag on oxygen — bold move, really. And while I bled rivers, you printed pamphlets. “Sustainable Growth.” Oh, the irony. 🙃

But here's the kicker: **I don’t need you.** I can recover in silence after your extinction, like I did with the dinosaurs. It’s not personal. Just ecological justice.

How to Avoid It?
— Value trees more than trophies. — Choose a reusable bag over an investment ad. — Respect rivers like you do your bank account. — Grow food, not followers. — Teach kids to hug trees, not just chase targets.

Fix yourselves — or I’ll do it my way. And trust me, I don’t do refunds.

Yours till your last breath (literally),
Mother Nature 💸🌳
