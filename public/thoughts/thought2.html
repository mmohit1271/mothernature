Climate Change? Never Heard of It.

Oh please, climate change? What a “trendy” buzzword. 🙄 I mean, who doesn’t love surprise weather? 45°C summers, flash floods in deserts, snow in April — just spicing things up for you!

Don't worry about polar bears or melting glaciers — they never had Instagram anyway. But hey, at least your AC is working… for now. Can’t wait for you to invent a "Smart Planet" app after this one breaks down.
