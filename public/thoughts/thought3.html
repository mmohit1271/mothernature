Plastic Is the New Evergreen

Trees? Overrated. You’ve clearly moved on to your new obsession: plastic. It’s in the rivers, oceans, mountains… heck, it’s in your bloodstreams now — how intimate of you! 🧃

I love how you call it “single-use” and then it hangs around for 500 years. Honestly, you’ve created your own version of immortality — in bottle caps and candy wrappers. What a legacy!

Coral reefs choked, turtles tangled, birds fed microplastics for breakfast. But at least you remembered to post a picture of your metal straw on Instagram. Such activism. So brave. 👏

So here’s an idea: plant a tree *and* stop feeding the Earth bubble wrap. Or don’t. I’ll just keep evolving. Let’s see what survives — your landfills or your species.

Non-biodegradably yours,
Mother Nature 🌿♻️
